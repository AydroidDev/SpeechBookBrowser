INTRODUCTION
How This Book Came to Be
In the early summer of 2004, I got a phone call from Steve Jobs.
He had been scattershot friendly to me over the years, with occa-
sional bursts of intensity, especially when he was launching a
new product that he wanted on the cover of Time or featured on
CNN, places where I’d worked. But now that I was no longer at
either of those places, I hadn’t heard from him much. We talked
a bit about the Aspen Institute, which I had recently joined, and I
invited him to speak at our summer campus in Colorado. He’d
be happy to come, he said, but not to be onstage. He wanted in-
stead to take a walk so that we could talk.
